About me

Hey there, my name is Robin. I'm a young designer and art director based in Düsseldorf, Germany. Find out more about me and the things that excite me.

About me

As a Graphic and Web Designer my main goal is to create the best possible experience for as many users as possible. I do this by aiming to break down a problem into its simplest form, examining alternate design paths and then executing the best solution. I excel at creating minimal, yet effective visual identities and digital solutions.

While I consider myself a designer first and foremost, I have experience with frontend development, SEO optimization and social media marketing. Constantly learning and experiencing new things. Other than that I'm passionate about photography, traveling, videogames and soccer.
